Project

Ensure

International Marketing Website

Role: Concept, IA, Design, Front-end Code

Brief

Abbott Nutrition asked us to re-design there international web presence for the Ensure nutritional product.

Solution

To deliver maximum value, effort was focused on three core tasks: content structuring, information architecture, and a clear set of overarching digital branding principles. This would enable the site to easily scale, but would also inform all other digital output that had previously suffered from a lack of consistency. Special attention was also given to the markup of the website to accomidate various different international language options.

A digital styleguide was created for the Abbott Ensure Team with the design tools necessary to maintain the website. As new content types are added or content is updated, thoughtful application of the details described in the styleguide will help maintain a consistent look and feel for the website.
